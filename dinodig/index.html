Localities, formations & specimens are real Alberta finds. Reveals now use field-safe fossil/reference images selected to avoid obvious people/reflections in the buried dig view. The Dinosaur Provincial Park Centrosaurus image has been replaced with Darren's clean Smithsonian/NMNH specimen image, and the Suncor/Borealopelta fossil view no longer uses the previous display-room photo. The registration panel still adds a second In life restoration image for each dinosaur; those images are paleoart/restorations, not museum display photos or mounted skeletons, and the life-image cards are set to contain the whole animal rather than crop it. Some fossil photos are casts or reference specimens rather than the exact individual from the field locality. The live map uses OpenStreetMap/CARTO tiles when available; the offline Alberta fallback is forced to keep its true aspect ratio so it does not stretch or skew. Sediment layers are simulated; depths are illustrative.

Image source note: Wikimedia Commons, Smithsonian/NMNH IDS, and credited science-news file links are listed in the PHOTOS, LIFE_IMAGES, PHOTO_CREDITS, and LIFE_CREDITS blocks. Check the source pages for exact attribution/license text before publishing outside the classroom.
