Magnetic Morphology
Flashcards

50+ Word Challenge (Grade 5)

Setup & How to Play

  1. Print Double-Sided: Set your printer to print on both sides, using "Flip on Short Edge" for landscape alignment.
  2. Cut the Cards: Cut along the dashed guide lines to make 52 individual flashcards.
  3. Build: Look at the solid color Word side, and build it using magnetic letters.
  4. Check: Flip the card over to check your Prefix, Root, Suffix, and meaning!

Ready to cut & play!
