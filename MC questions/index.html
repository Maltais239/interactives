From the Tree to a Sweet Treat

If you love chocolate, you're not alone—it's one of the world's most popular sweets. But did you know that chocolate comes from a plant? It's true—chocolate is made from the seeds of the cacao (kah KOW) tree. Cacao trees grow in areas close to the equator where temperatures are hot, such as parts of Africa, Central America, South America, and Asia.
