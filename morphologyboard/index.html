Magnetic
Morphology Boards

50+ Word Challenge (Grade 5)

How to Play

  1. Look at the solid color Word Card on the left.
  2. Use your magnetic letters to build the word on your cookie sheet board.
  3. Separate the word into its Prefix, Base/Root, and Suffix columns.
  4. Write the literal meaning at the bottom.
  5. Check your work against the Answer Card on the right!
