Create Your Own Flashcards

Enter your terms and definitions below, one per line. Use a colon ":" to separate them. The app will find a relevant emoji for each term. To customize an AI image instead, add details in parenthesis. For example:
Car (red sports car): A vehicle for driving.
For an artistic style, try: Planet (in the style of Van Gogh): ...).

Printing & Exporting

Once images are generated, you can save a print-ready file or export the images and data for other projects.
